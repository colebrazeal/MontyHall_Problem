Monty Hall Game Simulation

The Monty Hall game involves a contestant choosing one of three doors, behind one of which is a car and behind the others are goats. After the initial choice, the host, who knows what's behind the doors, opens one of the remaining doors to reveal a goat.

The contestant is then given the option to either stick with their original choice or switch to the other unopened door. The puzzle explores which strategy—sticking or switching—gives the contestant a better chance of winning the car.

This simple JavaScript function simulates a player scenario for that game returning the odd percentage of wins vs losses.
